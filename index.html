History of the Gardens

A city in a garden

Situated in the heart of Singapore and boasting a total land mass of 101 hectares, Gardens by the Bay is a sight to behold. Planning for the gardens first started in 2006, where more than 70 world-class design ideas from over 24 countries were sifted for the perfect master plan. Following intensive construction efforts, it's beauty was finally unveiled to the public in 2011.

Attractions

Bask in the great outdoors

So what exactly makes the gardens worth visiting? For starters, it has numerous attractions and exhibits that are open all year round! Whether its the mysterious allure of the Cloud Forest or the striking heights of the Supertree Observatory, there is always an attraction in store for everyone at the Gardens. Check out some of them below!

Where flowers never stop blooming

Gardens by the Bay's Flower Dome currently holds the Guinness World Record for the largest glass greenhouse! With an internal volume akin to 75 Olympic-sized swimming pools, this greenhouse is home to exotic plants from all over the Mediterranean and South Africa.

Escape into the surreal

Ever wanted to see amazing floral arrangements all year round? At Floral Fantasy, you can! Overhanging from the display's ceiling is a mix of over 15,000 fresh cut, dried and preserved blooms. Watch as they "dance" above your heads, displaying their cacophony of colors for all to see!

Have your head in the clouds

Step into the mist of the Cloud Forest, and be enthralled by one of the world's largest indoor waterfalls right before your eyes! In this greenhouse, keep your eyes peeled for carnivorous plants like the Venus Flytrap.You can also get amazing views of the entire conservatory with elevated walkways metres off the ground!

Events

Seize the season

Gardens by the Bay holds numerous, spectacular events throughout the year! These events are exceptionally unique, and always warrant a visit whenever they are held. Check out the variety of past events that were held in the Gardens below, and prepare yourself for those that have yet to come!

Christmas Wonderland Gardens by the Bay's 2021 Christmas Wonderland event featured a multitude of seasonal lighting installations and even an alfresco dining area!

Sakura X TokiDoki Fubuki, Ichiyo, Haru & Harumi, Yoshino and Sakurako – tokidoki’s five magical Unicornos – joins 2022's Sakura floral display in the Flower Dome!

Dahlia Dreams This year's Dahlia Dreams event during the Chinese New Year period featured cute coin pouches and tiger cub installations at the Flower Dome!
